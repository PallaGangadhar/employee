Policy Manual

EMS Manual Policy:

This guide is not a just a journal but a discipline & every team member must make sure that they follow company’s culture. We, at Narola believe in the culture of transparency & openness. You are in your own control & the focus is on getting stuff done quickly without wasting time on stuff that is not important.

Salary Processing:

Salary will be processed by 7th Date of every month.

Company Business Hours

  • Monday to Friday – 09:00 am to 06:00 pm
  • Lunch Break – 01:00 pm to 02:00 pm
  • Saturday – 09:00 am to 01:00 pm

Note – You are supposed to complete the assigned task on time. If necessary you will have to put extra efforts to finish the given task on time.

Late Marks:

  • > If anyone comes after 9:20 am, it will be considered as late mark.
  • > If anyone comes later than 2:05 PM from lunch, it will be considered as late mark.
  • > if anyone does not fill 8 hour a day, it will be considered as late mark.

Note – At the end of each month, three late marks will be considered as half day leave and will be deducted from salary.

Salary Slip FAQs:

  • > WD : Working Days
  • > WO : Week Off (Number of Sunday during Month)
  • > PH : Paid Holidays
  • > PD : Paid Days
  • > LWP : Leave Without Pay (Number of Approved + Un Approved Leaves)
  • > Basic Payable : Pay for the month after deduction of approved + un approved leaves
  • > HRA : Not Applicable
  • > Leave Encashment : [ (Salary / WD + PH) * 1.25 ]
  • > Production : Bonus
  • > Convenience : Phone Allowance / Other Allowance (To whomsoever it is applicable)
  • > Transport : Transport Allowance (To whomsoever it is applicable)
  • > P.F. : Provident Fund Deduction - Employee (12 % of Basic Payable) - To whomsoever it is applicable
  • > ESI : ESI Deduction - Employee [1.75% of (Basic Payable + Convenience Allowance + Transport + HRA + Leave Encashment) ] - To whomsoever it is applicable
    • * "April to Sep" and "Oct to Mar" - So if someone's salary is less than or equal to 21,000 in either of the slot for a single month, ESIC deduction will be there for entire slot
  • > P.T. : Professional Tax
  • > TDS : Income Tax
  • > Oth. Deduction : Late Mark Deduction + Snacks + Any Other Deduction

Note: Apart from it, all Saturdays will be counted as full day from 1st July, 2017, (Just for Total Monthly Working Days Calculations) even though we are working Half Day on Saturday. And if someone is taking leave on Saturday only half day salary will get deducted.

Leave Application Policy

You need take approval in advance if you plan for any leave except you have any health issue or any Urgent leave you take. For applying for Leave , you have to go on Leave Application Screen on Easycollab and apply for the leave.

>Unapproved Leaves

If you take leave without prior approval then it will be considered as unapproved leave and it will be deducted from salary. Unapproved leave will lead to a deduction of 1.5 days.

> Urgent Leaves

When any team member calls on the same day itself and informs that he/she won’t be able to come office on that particular day it will be considered as an Urgent leave reason. For example, Because of *sick leaves, Going out of station for urgent work, Going out of station for family issues

> Wedding Leave Policy

Narola Infotech (P) Ltd, allows total maximum 14 days (Including Weekend Off + Public Holiday) of leaves for wedding. In this 14 days you can plan all wedding + religious functions or going out of station. In case you need more leaves for going out of station then you must plan it after 3 months of your wedding leaves.

> Vacation Leave Policy

Narola Infotech (P) Ltd, allows total maximum 7 days (Including Week Off + Public Holiday) of leaves for yearly vacation. It is obvious that you should take prior approval for vacation leaves to manage work efficiently during your absence. Kindly inform well in advance.

Increment Policy

You will get yearly increment. If you join before 10th of any month, that month will be considered. (For example, if anyone joins on or after 11th of Jan, he/she will get his/her increment in Feb and if he/she joins on or before 10th of Jan, he/she will get increment in month of Jan itself)

Salary and Performance Reports

Salary hikes are on the bases of merit rather than experience alone. Performance of an employee is evaluated on the bases of etiquette, quality of code & productivity levels.
